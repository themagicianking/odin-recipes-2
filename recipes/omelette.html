Omelette

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • A handful of fresh basil
  • A dash of milk
  • 1/4 cup grated sharp cheddar
  • 1/4 cup rotissere chicken, chopped
  • Old Bay to taste
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter

    • Procedure

    Crack the eggs into a bowl and add a dash of milk and Old Bay. Stir well, making sure to break the yolks. Add the butter to the pan and let sit until melted. Pour in the eggs and then add the chicken. Stir until fluffy, then add cheese. Flip over and garnish with basil.
