Cinnamon Toast

Home

A simple breakfast I made for myself and my siblings when I was little.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 pieces of bread of your choice
  • A pinch of cinnamon
  • A pinch of sugar
  • A pat of butter

Steps

  1. Toast the bread until golden brown.
  2. Butter the bread.
  3. Sprinkle cinnamon and sugar over the toast.
